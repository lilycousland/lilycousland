Mowgli the cat

He is the cutest

The fact of the matter is that, Mowgli, though he cannot walk straight nor jump well, is the cutest, sweetest boy on the planet.

  • He snuggles like a teddy bear in the middle of the night
  • He makes cute sounds when he stretches his small body in the morning sunlight
  • He looks up into your eyes when he enters the room
  • He loves belly rubs

He is truly adorable.
